## Stitch It! Expands Manual Screenshot Stitching With Vertical and Horizontal Workflows

*The long-standing screenshot utility from Thirty Seven, Inc. continues to focus on precise, manual control for clean results.*

**San Francisco, February 2026** - Thirty Seven, Inc. shared a 2026 update for Stitch It!, its screenshot editing app for iOS and Android. Stitch It! helps people combine multiple screenshots into a single image with manual control over ordering, alignment, crop, and redaction.

### What Stitch It! Does

Stitch It! is designed for people who need dependable screenshot assembly without guesswork. Users can stitch image sequences manually, adjust placement, and export polished final images for sharing or documentation.

### Key Features

- Manual stitching workflow for precise control.
- Vertical and horizontal stitching modes.
- Drag-to-reorder arrangement before export.
- Redaction tools to hide names, faces, and sensitive details.
- Crop and trim controls for cleaner output.
- One-time Pro upgrade option for advanced usage.

### Typical Use Cases

- Sharing complete text or chat conversations.
- Building long-form screenshots for product support and QA.
- Combining research captures into one shareable reference image.
- Preparing clean visual receipts for internal documentation.
