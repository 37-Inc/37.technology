### What Stitch It! Does

Stitch It! automatically detects overlapping regions between screenshots and seamlessly stitches them into one continuous image. Users simply select their screenshots, and the app handles alignment, cropping, and blending. The result is a clean, scrollable image perfect for sharing conversations, articles, recipes, or any content that spans multiple screens.

### Key Features

- **Automatic stitching** — intelligent overlap detection combines screenshots with pixel-perfect accuracy
- **Manual arrangement** — drag and reorder screenshots when automatic detection isn't needed
- **Redaction tools** — blur or block out names, profile photos, or sensitive information before sharing
- **Crop and trim** — remove status bars, navigation elements, or unwanted edges
- **Cross-platform** — available on iOS, Android, and web (stitch-it.app)
